To use randomate enter a word or phrase into the top right search box, if you want, you can also include a number of items you want. Then press the search button and receive a list of playlists to discover! To see the playlist description hover over "...". To see the spotify page for that playlist, click on the heart button to the left of the playlist name. If you would like to see the tracks of a specific playlist click on the playlist.

Quick Guide...

Welcome to Randomate!

Randomate makes discovering new music quick and easy! The only limitation is your imagination. Finding new music can now be done in four easy steps.

  1. Enter a word or phrase into the search bar
  2. Click the search button
  3. Browse the suggested playlists
  4. Enjoy new music!

To the right you can see the top tracks of the US! If you would like to see another county's top tracks select a country in the drop down menu in the section below.

Interested in another Country's Top Tracks?

Choose a country from the menu below and click the search button!

You have three options! You can choose a country and enter a number. Enter only a country. Or enter nothing at all and simply enjoy the US tracks!
