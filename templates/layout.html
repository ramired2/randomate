To use randomate enter a word or phrase into the top right search box, then press go and receive a list of playlists to discover! To see the playlist description hover over "...". To see the spotify page for that playlist, click on the heart button to the left of the playlist name. If you would like to see the tracks of a specific playlist click on the playlist.
